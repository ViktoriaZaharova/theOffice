Information

"The Office" is the largest and contemporary co-working center and boutique offices in Azerbaijan - the new path of melding life and work in a sustainable fashion, which is located in the heart of downtown, nearby Nizami Cinema Center, It is inalso between “28 May” and “Sahil” metro stations.

Information

Our creative co-working spaces and full-furnished boutique offices with office facilities inclusive (meeting rooms, lounges, internet, printer, scanner, reception, tea, coffee etc.) are designed to provide a productive and collaborative environment for our dynamic inhabitants, and created without corporate constraints on what is perceived to be an “office” environment offering flexible membership to meet your needs.

Information

But, “TheOffice” co-working and boutique offices center is not just a space for entrepreneurs, startups and innovators. We are a community that promises to our residents to create the modern ways of working together, collaborate with one another and surround themselves with other brilliant minds.

Information

“TheOffice” connects you to a global network of professionals and offers you good service and the feeling of belonging to a certain community.

We would love to have you as a member of our community!
