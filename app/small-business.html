Small Business & Start-Up Essentials Workshop

  • Organizer: Law Offices of Pardalis & Nohavicka, LLP
  • Price: FREE

DESCRIPTION

Join us at WeWork 750 Lexington to learn how to best protect your small business, start-up or brand:

  • Interactive Legal Workshop on protecting your company's intellectual property & brand
  • Specialized advice for Business & Start-Up Owners
  • Dynamic Q&A Session
  • Complimentary Food & Drinks
  • Networking Hour with over 150 NYC Professionals
  • Personalized 1 - on - 1 Follow up Complimentary Legal Consultations
DETAILED LEGAL WORKSHOP BREAKDOWN

  • 1. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY DISCUSSION

    What, When, How: Trademarks, Copyrights, Patents. A talk on ownership of IP throughout the term of development.

  • 2. PROTECTING YOUR COMPANY AND ITS INFORMATION

    How to protect your company's proprietary info including trade secrets, code and customer or user lists through the use of contracts.

  • 3. LABOR AND EMPLOYMENT

    What to do when hiring your first employee. Going over the Employee vs. Independent Contractor Dilemma

  • 4. MARKETING & OUTREACH

    How to utilize social media, networking, and WeWork's Global Network for instant business growth.
